# BIP-0148: Mandatory activation of segwit deployment

## Summary

This document specifies a BIP16 like soft fork flag day activation of the segregated witness BIP9 deployment known as "segwit".

## Body

**Typ:** Specification  
**Layer:** Consensus (soft fork)  
**Status:** Deployed (aktiviert und im Einsatz)  
**Autor(en):** Shaolin Fry
