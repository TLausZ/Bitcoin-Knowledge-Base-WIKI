# BIP-0329: Wallet Labels Export Format

## Summary

This document specifies a format for the export of labels that may be attached to various common types of records in a wallet.

## Body

**Typ:** Informational  
**Layer:** Applications  
**Status:** Draft (noch im Entwurfsstadium)  
**Themen:** bips, self-custody
**Autor(en):** Craig Raw  
**Veröffentlicht:** 2022-08-23  
**Quelle:** https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0329.mediawiki

### Zweck

Wallet labels — notes on addresses ("Cold storage"), transactions ("Payment to landlord"), or UTXOs ("Do not spend — tainted") — are among the most valuable data in a Bitcoin wallet. They represent the user's understanding of their coin history and are essential for coin control and privacy. Currently, labels are wallet-specific: switching from Sparrow to Electrum means losing all labels.

BIP-329 proposes a standardised JSONL export format covering labels for addresses, transactions, UTXOs, xpubs, and transaction input/output indices. Each record is a JSON object with `type`, `ref` (the item identifier), `label`, and optional `origin` and `spendable` fields.

Authored by Craig Raw (creator of Sparrow Wallet), the format has been implemented in Sparrow and a few other wallets. As a Draft, formal standardisation is incomplete, but practical adoption is ahead of BIP status.
