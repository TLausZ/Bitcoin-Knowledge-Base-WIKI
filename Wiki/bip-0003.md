### Zweck

BIP-0003 is the current BIP process document as of 2025. Written by Murch, it replaces BIP-2 and brings several concrete updates: BIP numbers are now assigned when a proposal is merged into the repository (not just accepted), the champion role is more explicitly defined, and the status model is simplified. It also removes the controversial concept of "Final" status for consensus-layer BIPs, since deployed consensus rules can never truly be considered finalized as long as nodes run them.

The document requires BIPs to include a "Deployment" section describing how the change is expected to be activated — making the activation path explicit rather than left to later discussion. This was a direct response to the SegWit activation disputes of 2017.
