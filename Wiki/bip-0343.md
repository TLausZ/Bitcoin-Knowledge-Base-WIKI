# BIP-0343: Mandatory activation of taproot deployment

## Summary

This document specifies a BIP-8 (LOT=true) deployment to activate Taproot.

## Body

**Typ:** Specification  
**Layer:** Consensus (soft fork)  
**Status:** Closed (geschlossen (zurückgezogen oder ersetzt))  
**Themen:** bips, privacy
**Autor(en):** Shinobius, Michael Folkson  
**Veröffentlicht:** 2021-04-25  
**Quelle:** https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0343.mediawiki

### Zweck

BIP-0343 proposed activating Taproot via BIP-8 with `LOT=true` (Locked-In On Timeout). Under LOT=true, if miners didn't signal the required threshold within the signalling window, activation would lock in regardless — a UASF-style forced activation similar in spirit to BIP-148 for SegWit.

The proposal reflected community division over activation strategy: some wanted Speedy Trial (a short, low-threshold signalling window), while others wanted LOT=true as a credible threat to ensure miners couldn't block activation indefinitely.

In practice, Taproot activated via "Speedy Trial" (Bitcoin Core's approach) in November 2021: a 3-month BIP-9-style signalling window with a 90% threshold and a defined lock-in date. Miners achieved the threshold in June 2021, and Taproot locked in before any forced-activation mechanism was needed. BIP-343 was closed as moot.
