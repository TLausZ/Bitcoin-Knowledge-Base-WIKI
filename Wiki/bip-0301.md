# BIP-0301: Blind Merged Mining (Consensus layer)

## Summary

Blind Merged Mining allows SHA-256 miners to collect transaction fee revenue from sidechains without running any sidechain software. Sidechain users bid on mainchain to win the right to create sidechain blocks.

### Zweck

BIP-0301 provides the block production mechanism for BIP-300 sidechains. Traditional merged mining (as on Namecoin) requires miners to run the sidechain node software to produce sidechain blocks. "Blind" merged mining removes this requirement: sidechain participants bid BTC on mainchain for the right to determine the next sidechain block. The winning bidder's chosen block hash is included in the Bitcoin coinbase. Miners collect the BTC bid without knowing anything about the sidechain.

This allows sidechain fee revenue to flow to Bitcoin miners without increasing miner infrastructure requirements. From the miner's perspective, it's just another transaction fee. From the sidechain user's perspective, their desired sidechain block creator wins a simple auction on mainchain.

BIP-301 is tightly coupled with BIP-300 — BMM handles block production, hashrate escrows handle withdrawal security. Both remain Draft and contested.
