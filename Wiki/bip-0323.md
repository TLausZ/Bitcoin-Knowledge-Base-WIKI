# BIP-0323: 24 nVersion bits for general purpose use

## Summary

24 bits are reserved in the nVersion field as extra nonce space for miners, enabling additional hashrate without relying on rolling nTime more than once per second.

### Zweck

BIP-0323 extends BIP-320's 16-bit nVersion reservation to 24 bits, providing miners with more nonce space in the block header for efficient mining without changing the coinbase transaction. At modern hashrates, ASICs exhaust even 16 extra bits quickly; 24 bits extends the header search space to 16 million additional values per nonce cycle.

The 2026 draft date reflects ongoing work to accommodate increasingly powerful mining hardware. The reserved bits are explicitly excluded from BIP-9 soft fork signalling interpretation, so mining with these bits set doesn't accidentally signal activation of a non-existent fork.
