# BIP-0361: Post Quantum Migration and Legacy Signature Sunset

## Summary

This proposal follows the implementation of any post-quantum (PQ) output type and introduces a pre-announced sunset of legacy ECDSA/Schnorr signatures. It turns quantum security into a private incentive: fail to upgrade and you will encounter additional friction to access your funds, creating a certainty where none previously existed. Phase A: Disallows sending of any funds to quantum-vulnerable addresses, hastening the adoption of PQ address types. Phase B: Renders ECDSA/Schnorr spends invalid, preventing all spending of funds in quantum-vulnerable UTXOs.
