# BIP-0197: Hashed Time-Locked Collateral Contract

## Summary

This BIP describes a script for generalized debt agreement contract based on Hashed Time-Lock Contract (BIP 199) transactions according to the Atomic Loans specification. A Hashed Time-Locked Collateral Contract (HTLCC) enables cross-chain collateralized loans where Bitcoin serves as collateral for a loan denominated in another blockchain's currency.
