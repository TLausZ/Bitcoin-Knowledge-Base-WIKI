# BIP-0175: Pay to Contract Protocol

**Status:** speculative
**Last updated:** 2026-06-07
**Sources:** [[bip-0175]]

Layer: Applications · Typ: Informational · Status: Closed · Datum: 2017-07-17
**Autoren:** Omar Shibli, Nicholas Gregory

## Summary

Utilizing hierarchical deterministic wallets as described in BIP-0032 and the "Purpose Field" in BIP-0043, this document specifies the multiparty pay-to-contract key derivation scheme outlined by Ilja Gerhardt and Timo Hanke.
