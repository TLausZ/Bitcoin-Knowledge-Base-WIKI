## Summary

Although it is widely believed that Satoshi was an inflation-hating goldbug he never said this, and in fact programmed Bitcoin's money supply to grow indefinitely, forever. He modeled the monetary supply as 4 gold mines being discovered per mibillennium (1024 years), with equal intervals between them, each one being depleted over the course of 140 years. This poses obvious problems, however.
