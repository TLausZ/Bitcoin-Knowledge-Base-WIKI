## Summary

As the Bitcoin network scales, roles are fast becoming specialised. In the beginning, a single Bitcoin user would perform the synonymous roles of miner, merchant and end-user. With the growth however of this system, these functions are being abstracted away to specialised services as a natural part of Bitcoin's growth. Bitcoin's blockchain becomes more unwieldy for end users over time, negatively affecting the usability of Bitcoin clients.
