# BIP-0339: WTXID-based transaction relay

**Status:** established
**Last updated:** 2026-06-08
**Sources:** [[bip-0339]]

Layer: Peer Services · Typ: Specification · Status: Deployed · Datum: 2020-02-03
**Autoren:** Suhas Daftuar <sdaftuar@chaincode.com>

## Summary

This BIP describes two changes to the p2p protocol to support transaction relay based on the BIP 141 wtxid of a transaction, rather than its txid.
