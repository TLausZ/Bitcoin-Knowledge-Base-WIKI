# BIP-0109: Two million byte size limit with sigop and sighash limits

**Status:** speculative
**Last updated:** 2026-06-07
**Sources:** [[bip-0109]]

Layer: Consensus (hard fork) · Typ: Specification · Status: Closed · Datum: 2016-01-28
**Autoren:** Gavin Andresen

## Summary

One-time increase in total amount of transaction data permitted in a block from 1MB to 2MB, with limits on signature operations and hashing.
